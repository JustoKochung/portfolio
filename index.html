ICT Project Term 2

This is a capstone project, which is part of formative assessment for ICT Integration in Education as a learning area leading to the award of a Diploma in Secondary Teacher Education. In this project, i leveraged on the power of Excel, Powepoint, Google meet, GitHub, VsCode, & Copilot to showcase my digital skills for a CBC competent teacher. The ICT in learning project is applicable for Schools, teachers, learning institutions, and governments aiming to introduce digital skills in learning.
